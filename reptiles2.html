OUR MISSION

The MYZOO provides the community with a unique educational entertainment facility which houses over 100 exotic species of reptiles, amphibians, and arachnids from around the world.

What To Expect When Visiting The Reptile Zoo


In anticipation of visiting The Reptile Zoo and to support efforts to help reduce the spread of the virus within the community, we have implemented modifications. Please carefully read all guidelines and information at the link to prepare for new procedures for visiting. The health and safety of our guests, employees, volunteers and the more than 100 animals in our care remain our top priority and we thank you for helping us with these efforts.

OUR REPTILE FAMILY

I have powerful jaws with many conical teeth and short legs with clawed webbed toes. I have a unique body form that allows my eyes, ears, and nostrils to be above the water surface .

I am a reptile with hard shells that protects me from predators. I am the oldest and most primitive groups of reptiles, having evolved millions of years ago.I spend most of my life in water.

I am a reptile that are part of the iguana suborder.I can change my skin color so I am also called colorful lizard. However, it is a misconception that I change colors to match my surroundings.

I am also called serpent,distinguished by limbless condition and greatly elongated body and tail.I lack movable eyelids and external ear openings.

I am scaly-skinned reptiles that are usually distinguished from snakes by the possession of legs, movable eyelids, and external ear openings.

I am the member of the order Crocodylia.I have black skin, rounded snouts and only top teeth visible when my mouth is closed.

I belong to the family Testudinidae of the order Testudines.I am particularly distinguished from other turtles by being exclusively land-dwelling, while many other turtle species are at least partly aquatic.

I may look like a lizard, but I am unique. I am not a lizard; I am the only living member of the order Rhynchocephalia, which flourished around 200 million years ago.

I am a small,carnivorous reptile with soft skin that have a wide distribution. I also possess a short stout body, a large head, and typically well-developed limbs. ... I have feet modified for climbing.
